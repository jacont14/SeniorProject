Landscape Services

Yard Landscaping Maintenance

We offer regular maintenance service of either every two weeks from the date that we started or We can also offer a monthly or every two months maintenance and every month or if you decide two we can contact you to schedule a day for service and We could also offer one time yard service. Maintenance will include:

  • bush trimming
  • blowing trash and leaves
  • weeds removal
  • spraying for weeds
  • mowing lawn
  • trimming palms

Tree trimming

Are your trees getting overgrown? we can trim the tree bak and shape up the tree and trim the tree to your liking. We trim all different types and sized tree and We haul away all the tree limbs, branches and trash from the tree.

Tree Removal

If you have a tree that you are no longer wanting in your yard or have a tree that has fallen over we can remove the tree for you and we can give you a quote on it. We can either cut down the tree all the way down or if you were wanting we can fully remove it out with the roots and we haul away everything as well.

irrigation repair and Installation

We do irrigation repairs and installations such as:

  • irrigation pipe leaks
  • valve repairs and installation
  • sprinkler repair and installation
  • irrigation wire repair
  • install drip system
  • install pvc or regular irrigation pipe
  • irrigation timer

Planting

We can plant any type of plants from 1 gallon up to 5 gallon plants in the area that you would like and if it doesn't have a drip, we can install a drip for the plant to get water. We will also apply special dirt and fertilizer for the plants.

Palm Trimming

Are your palm tress overgrowing and are in a need of trimming, we trim the palms and all different sizes.

Lawn Maintenance

We maintain your lawn by mowing it and we can go every two weeks or once a week to mow the lawn to ensure that you grass is nicely cut and maintained.

Weed Removal & Spray For Weeds

Is your yard getting filled with weeds? We remove remove and spray the weeds that are in your yard to ensure that there isn't more growth of weeds in your yard.

Grass Removal

If you are no longer wanting grass on your yard here at AZ landscaping we remove grass.

Spreading Gravel

Here in AZ Landscaping we apply and spread gravel on your yard from quarter inch, half, to 1 inch sized rock. I currently charge per ton to spread the gravel and I could order the gravel for it be delivered or you could order it. We then can contact you to pick a date for us to go and spread the gravel.

Grass Seeding

If your grass isn't growing or it has patches, I can apply seeding for the grass. I also do seasonal seeding for both the summer and the winter grass. I normally buy the seed for the grass and charge for applying and buying the seed.
